Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and more.

General Services

What services does SnowBaseStudio offer?

We specialize in custom website design, AI-powered web solutions, UX/UI design, web development, and ongoing site maintenance.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Yes! We support startups, small businesses, and large enterprises alike.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely. We offer website revamps to modernize your look and improve performance.

Do you build e-commerce websites?

Yes, we can create secure, scalable online stores tailored to your business needs.

What technologies do you use?

Our stack includes Python, SQL, React, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML5, Node.js, and CSS3.

Project Process

How does the design process work?

We start with a ideas session, move to wireframes and prototypes, and then build your site with your feedback and input at every step.

How long does it take to build a website?

Typical projects take 1–6 weeks, depending on complexity and requirements.

Can I track project progress?

Yes, we provide regular updates and milestones so you're always informed.

Do I own my website after it's completed?

Yes, you receive full ownership of your website and its assets.

Pricing & Payment

How much does a website cost?

Pricing depends on your needs and project scope. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we can split payments across project milestones.

Are there any ongoing costs?

Ongoing costs may include hosting, maintenance, or optional feature upgrades.

Technical & Support

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Upon request, sites can be fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices.

Can you integrate third-party tools or APIs?

Yes, we can connect your website to CRMs, payment gateways, analytics, and more.

Security & Privacy

How do you handle my data and privacy?

We take data protection seriously and comply with all relevant regulations. Your data is only used for project purposes and never shared without consent.

Getting Started

How do I start a project with SnowBaseStudio?

Simply reach out via our contact form or email. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your goals.

What do I need to provide before starting?

Any branding materials, content, or ideas you have. We'll guide you through the rest!
